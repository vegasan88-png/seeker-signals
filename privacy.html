Seeker Radar Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Seeker Radar is a mobile app that helps users discover Solana-related opportunities, dApps, quests, news, and wallet-based signals. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes and how it is used.

1. Information we collect or process

Seeker Radar does not require users to create an account. The app may process the following information:

  • Public wallet address: if you connect a Solana wallet, the app may read your public wallet address and public blockchain data associated with that address, such as token balances or activity visible on Solana.
  • App interaction data: the app may store local preferences or cached app data on your device to improve performance and avoid repeated network requests.
  • Network request data: when the app loads signals, news, dApp information, wallet balances, or token metadata, requests may be sent to third-party services such as Supabase, Solana RPC providers, and Helius.

2. Wallets, private keys, and custody

Seeker Radar is non-custodial. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or have access to your seed phrase, private keys, or recovery phrase. Wallet connection and signing are handled by the wallet or wallet infrastructure available on your device. Any transaction or signature request must be reviewed and approved by you through your wallet.

3. How we use information

Information processed by the app is used to:

  • show wallet-related information such as balances, tokens, and public activity;
  • display relevant Solana ecosystem opportunities, dApps, quests, and news;
  • open external links selected by the user;
  • reduce duplicate content and repeated network requests;
  • improve app reliability and prevent excessive API usage or rate-limit errors.

4. Third-party services

Seeker Radar may use third-party services to provide app functionality, including:

  • Supabase: used to provide app content such as signals, news, or dApp metadata.
  • Solana RPC providers and Helius: used to read public Solana blockchain information.
  • External dApp and news websites: opened only when the user chooses to open an external link.

These services may process technical information such as IP address, request metadata, device/network information, and public wallet address when required to serve requests. Their processing is governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Local storage

The app may store cached data, settings, or temporary information locally on your device. This data is used for app functionality and performance. You can remove locally stored app data by clearing the app storage or uninstalling the app.

6. Analytics and advertising

Seeker Radar does not currently use advertising identifiers and does not sell user data. If analytics or advertising features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are used.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share or process information only when necessary to operate the app, comply with legal obligations, prevent abuse, or provide services requested by the user.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect app functionality and configuration. However, public blockchain data is inherently public, and no networked service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. User choices

You may use parts of the app without connecting a wallet. You can disconnect your wallet through your wallet app, clear local app data, or uninstall Seeker Radar at any time.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “Last updated” date.
